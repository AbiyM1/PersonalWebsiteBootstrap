Cyber Security national competition and training.

A few words about the experience I gained through this program.

Cyber security is basically the processes and technologies designed to defend against threats on a network, protect; devices, programs and data from attackers or unauthorized access.

If you are not new to this subject, you'd probably guess that it's a rapidly growing profession. And since there wasn't much I had to do during quarantine, I wanted to take my hobby to the next level.

I still am a rookie, but I was lucky enough to enroll in the 6 day free training offered by Cybertalents and it was fantastic.

Our class sessions were made through Zoom and lasted 4 hours each day and the course was content rich. We had an excellent mentor and he went through every detail.

We learned from setting up virtual machines to web-application penetration testing to malware reverse engineering. Even though me and my team didn't make it to the top 3 finalists, it was a worthy experience.
